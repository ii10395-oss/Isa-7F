Real Madrid CF

The Most Successful Football Club in the World

Club History & Stadium

Founded in 1902 as Madrid Football Club, the team has traditionally worn a white home kit since inception. The word "Real" is Spanish for Royal. Interestingly, Madrid is also home to the [Sobrino de Botín](https://www.casabotin.com), founded in 1725, which is the oldest working restaurant in the world according to the Guinness Book of Records.

Honours & Records

  • Champions League: 15 Titles
  • La Liga: 36 Championships
  • Copa del Rey: 20 Titles
  • UEFA Cup: 2 Titles (1985, 1986)

Legendary Players

Cristiano Ronaldo

Cristiano Ronaldo is widely considered Real Madrid's best player ever. He netted a record 450 goals in 438 appearances, averaging over one goal per game during his nine seasons (2009–2018). He was the key catalyst in winning four Champions League titles and securing four of his five Ballon d'Or awards while at the club.
